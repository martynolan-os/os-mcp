Frequently Asked Questions

What is MCP?
MCP stands for Model Context Protocol. It is the connection between your Openstage fan data and the AI you already use, like Claude. Connect once, then ask any question about your fans in plain English and get real answers back.
Do I need a developer?
No. Generate a key in Artist Settings, paste it at the authorize page, copy one command. Under two minutes.
Which AI tools work with it?
MCP is an open standard, so it works with any AI tool that supports it. Claude (desktop and browser), Claude Code, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Cursor, Windsurf, Perplexity, and more. If your tool speaks MCP, it works. Claude is the most popular starting point, and connecting takes about two minutes.
Which plans include it?
Build and Legendary.
What does it cost?
No extra Openstage charge. You just need your own Claude subscription.
